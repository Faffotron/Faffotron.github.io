About

I graduated from Victoria University of Wellington in 2020 with a Bachelor of Design Innovation, majoring in Media Design and specialising in Creative Coding, Game Design, and Interactive Design.

Most of my recent projects have been in Rust and GLSL, but I am most familiar with C#, Javascript, and Processing/Java. I also dabble in C, Inform 7, LaTeX, Python, and Bash, among others.

I use Blender for 3D work, Neovim for programming and writing, Aseprite for pixel work, and Affinity Designer for my vector art and typography needs.
