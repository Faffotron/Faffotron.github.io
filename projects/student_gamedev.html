Student Gamedev Work

While studying Media Design at Victoria University of Wellington I worked on some solo and group game projects.

Hortus Hyalus

This was a solo project for a Computer Graphics class. A small toroidal world is generated which the user can manipulate by moving tiles with their cursor. Plants slowly grow and spread across the landscape while small flittering creatures wander, graze on vegetation, reproduce, and eventually die.

Heliostar

A solo project for my Game Design classes. The player's green spaceship must evade pursuing red enemy ships around a swirling black hole in the middle of the play space, luring pursuers into being destroyed by the player's engine trail or the black hole.

Knave

A platformer made as a group project for my Game Design classes. The player controls a cowled character to navigate and escape from a castle's dungeon, acquiring abilities as they explore which allow them to overcome previously impassable obstacles and evade enemies. I was responsible for programming the sprite animation system, character and object physics, enemy AI, camera point of interest system, and object interactions.
